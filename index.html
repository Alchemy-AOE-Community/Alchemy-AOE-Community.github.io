About Us

The Alchemy AOE Community is a free and non-profit organization of random map scripters, tournament hosts, content creators, and skilled players dedicated to the advancement of custom random maps in Age of Empires II: Definitive Edition. We publish quality standards for custom random maps, and curate the finished products to be featured in thematic competitions where adventuresome players may exhibit creative strategies for maximum entertainment.
